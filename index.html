In all marketing plans it is important to set the objectives that will guide your strategy and social media marketing is no exception. Create Quality Content

The first step to creating quality content is to identify what type of information will be useful to your audience. Remember that your content must be entertaining and visually pleasing, as users will look at all these details to evaluate it. Also, do not forget that users do not like to be saturated with the same information, much less if it is only information from your company. Give them something that interests them and above all that is useful.

Identify Your Audience

You must identify who you want to address on social networks as they are an essential part of your strategy. Remember that you must build your buyer persona not only through assumptions but also through research.
