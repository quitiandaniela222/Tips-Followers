Companies that have been able to take advantage of social networks in very different sectors. Because it doesn't matter so much what sector your business is in, it matters to detect your target audience and offer them the content they demand on each of the platforms.

Stories

The concept of "virality" in networks is something sought by many but achieved by few. This does not mean that any user cannot achieve it but, in addition to meeting certain social factors,
it is very important that we have a certain degree of "luck" in this regard.

Ester Expósito


    Known for her role in the Netflix series "Elite." Who was going to tell this girl that, after posting a dancing video on her Instagram account, it would be a success on this social
    network and would become the most viewed in the world. We leave the post below so you can take a look.


    In just a few days, this publication of Ester dancing reggaeton in her room during the quarantine accumulated millions of views and is currently about to reach 95 million, in addition
    to 11 million likes and 300,000 comments. As she commented in her interview on the La Resistencia program, she herself did not expect such a simple video to have such an impact. She
    herself commented on said program that she longed to be able to party with her friends and for this reason, together with the boredom of confinement due to COVID-19, was why she made the publication.
    But at no time did I expect the post to go as far as it has managed to do.


After this publication, if we take a look at his statistics from the SocialBlade website, his account has exploded on Instagram, reaching almost 30 million followers. This tool allows us to know the flow of followers,
among other statistics of certain social accounts on the Internet, but normally, we do not find growth of this type no matter how much we investigate through it. So, in addition to having the most viewed video on this app, it is also the Spanish profile with the most followers on Instagram.
